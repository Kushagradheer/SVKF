About Us

Founded in 2013, Shree Visatkrupa Shroff and Finance (SVKF) has established itself as a trusted name in project finance and construction. With over a decade of experience, we have successfully managed and financed numerous construction projects, helping our clients achieve their goals with precision and efficiency.
In January 2024, we expanded our horizons by venturing into the timber trading industry, specializing in the export of high-quality teak, pine, and lumber wood from Africa. Our timber is sourced from sustainable forests and is ideal for furniture making, meeting the highest standards of quality and durability.

Our Services

Teak Lumber Export

At SVKF, we specialize in exporting premium quality teak lumber sourced from sustainable forests in Africa. Our teak lumber is renowned for its durability, aesthetic appeal, and suitability for high-end furniture and construction projects.

Teak Logs Export

Our teak logs are meticulously selected to ensure the highest quality. SVKF provides robust teak logs that are perfect for various applications, from furniture making to architectural uses, offering excellent resistance to weathering and decay.

Timber Lumber Export

Our timber lumber is processed to meet the highest standards of quality and precision. SVKF ensures that our lumber is ideal for construction, carpentry, and furniture making, offering superior strength and consistency.

Project Financing

SVKF offers tailored project finance solutions to help our clients bring their visions to life. With a deep understanding of the financial landscape, we provide comprehensive support to ensure your projects are funded efficiently and effectively.

Timber Logs Export

SVKF's timber logs are sourced from sustainable forests, providing our clients with high-quality raw materials for construction and manufacturing. These logs are versatile and ideal for a wide range of industrial applications.

Building Construction

With a decade of experience, SVKF excels in delivering high-quality building construction services. Our team of experts ensures that every project is executed with precision, adhering to the highest standards of safety and craftsmanship.

Why Choose Us

At SVKF, we prioritize quality above all else. Our teak and timber products undergo rigorous selection and quality control processes to ensure they meet the highest standards. We source our materials from sustainable and responsible forestry practices, ensuring that our products not only enhance your projects but also contribute to environmental stewardship.
